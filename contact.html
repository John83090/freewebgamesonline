Frequently Asked Questions

Are all games completely free? ▼
Yes! Every game on FreeWebGamesOnline is 100% free to play with no downloads, no sign-ups, and no in-app purchases. We're supported by Google AdSense advertisements.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No account needed! Your game progress and high scores are saved automatically in your browser's local storage. This means your saves stay private and on your device.
Can I suggest a new game? ▼
Absolutely! We love hearing game requests. Email us or use the contact form above with your suggestion. We regularly add new games based on user requests.
Why do I see ads on the site? ▼
Ads help us cover hosting costs and keep the games free for everyone. We use Google AdSense which serves relevant, safe ads. You can opt out of personalized ads at google.com/settings/ads.
A game is not working — what should I do? ▼
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, please contact us with the game name and your browser/device info so we can fix it quickly.
