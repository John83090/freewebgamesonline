Design Rationale · Why This Ranks
✦ Bold Wordmark (Roblox strategy)
The name IS the logo. Heavy 900-weight type is instantly readable at any size, matching Roblox's approach of letting the name carry the brand.
✦ Distinctive Icon (CrazyGames strategy)
A clean game controller in a gradient rounded square — immediately signals "games" without ambiguity. Works as favicon, app icon, and avatar.
✦ Color Split (Poki strategy)
"Web" in purple/blue gradient pops out — the eye reads "Free•Web•Games" in a rhythm. Creates visual hierarchy without extra design elements.
✦ "ONLINE" label (differentiator)
Spaced-out caps below the main name — like a product line label. Signals this is the online/web version. Borrowed from premium brand typography.
